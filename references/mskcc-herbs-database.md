# Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center (MSKCC) About Herbs Database

## Overview

The **MSKCC About Herbs, Botanicals & Other Products** database is a premier evidence-based resource for integrative medicine information, specifically designed for cancer patients and healthcare professionals.

## Access Information

**Database URL**: https://www.mskcc.org/cancer-care/diagnosis-treatment/symptom-management/integrative-medicine/herbs

**Access Type**: Freely accessible to the public and healthcare professionals

**Mobile App**: Available for iOS and Android devices for convenient access

## Database Characteristics

### Management & Oversight
- Managed by a **pharmacist and botanicals expert**
- Developed with assistance from the Integrative Medicine Service
- Provides **objective, evidence-based** information

### Content Coverage
The database includes:
- **Herbs and botanicals** (A-Z listing)
- **Dietary supplements**
- **Other natural products**
- Information on traditional uses, potential benefits, adverse effects, and **drug interactions**

## Key Features

### 1. Evidence-Based Summaries
Each entry provides:
- Scientific and common names
- Traditional uses
- Clinical evidence summary
- Reported side effects
- Known drug interactions
- Special considerations for cancer patients

### 2. Search Capabilities
- **Alphabetical browsing** (A-Z index)
- **Keyword search** functionality
- **Category filters** for different types of supplements

### 3. Professional Oversight
The pharmacist-managed editorial process ensures:
- Accuracy of pharmacological information
- Clinical relevance for cancer care
- Appropriate citation of sources

## How to Use This Database in Clinical Practice

### Step 1: Initial Search
Access the database at the URL above and use the search function to find the herb or supplement of interest.

### Step 2: Review Summary
Read the evidence summary which includes:
- What the herb is traditionally used for
- What clinical studies show
- Current evidence quality

### Step  3: Check Interactions
Review the "Interactions" section which categorizes interactions as:
- **Drug Interactions**
- **Herb-Drug Interactions**
- **Food Interactions**

### Step 4: Special Cancer Considerations
Look for specific notes about:
- Effects on chemotherapy
- Effects on radiation therapy
- Effects on blood counts
- Surgical considerations

## Important Caveat

> "The United States Food and Drug Administration (FDA) does not evaluate the safety and labeling of dietary supplements before they are sold."

This statement from MSKCC highlights the importance of consulting authoritative databases like About Herbs when patients are considering supplements.
